Cribbage Trainer is a web application designed to teach both the fundamentals and nuances of the card game Cribbage. It includes both a guided tutorial for learning to score cribbage hands, as well as a game mode that goes deep into the probabilities and outcomes of different player choices. Cribbage Trainer was an exploration in designing algorithms, serving the results in an API, and packaging the whole thing into an application that is fun, intuitive, and informative.
